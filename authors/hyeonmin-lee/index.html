I am an incoming Assistant Professor at Hanyang University ERICA, starting in Spring 2026. I earned my Ph.D. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University, where I was co-advised by Prof. Taekyoung “Ted” Kwon (Seoul National University) and Prof. Taejoong “Tijay” Chung (Viginia Tech). Before joining Hanyang University, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Seoul National University (with Prof. Taekyoung “Ted” Kwon) and at the University of Virginia (with Prof. Yixin Sun).

[ANNOUNCEMENT] · Jul 4, 2025
I will be joining Hanyang University ERICA as an Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Science, College of Computing, starting in Spring 2026.

Research Interests

  • Key Topics: Domain Name System (DNS), Public Key Infrastructure (PKI), Transport Layer Security (TLS), and Email security.
