Hyeonmin Lee

Network Security Researcher

Computer Science

University of Virginia (UVA)

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia, working with Prof. Yixin Sun. I earned my Ph.D. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University, where I was co-advised by Prof. Taekyoung “Ted” Kwon (Seoul National University) and Prof. Taejoong “Tijay” Chung (Viginia Tech).

[ANNOUNCEMENT] I will be joining Hanyang University ERICA as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Science, College of Computing, starting in Spring 2026.

Research Interests

  • Network Security: Securing Internet infrastructure through data-driven security analysis, protocol development and optimization, and threat mitigation.
  • Key Topics: Domain Name System (DNS), Public Key Infrastructure (PKI), Transport Layer Security (TLS), and Email security.
