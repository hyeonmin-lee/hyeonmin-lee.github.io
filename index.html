Hyeonmin Lee

Network Security Researcher

Computer Science

University of Virginia (UVA)

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia, working with Prof. Yixin Sun. I received my Ph.D. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University, where I was mentored by Prof. Taekyoung “Ted” Kwon (Seoul National University) and Prof. Taejoong “Tijay” Chung (Viginia Tech).

Research Interests

  • Network Security: I am interested in topics such as, but not limited to, the Domain Name System (DNS), Transport Layer Security (TLS)/Public Key Infrastructure (PKI), and Email.
  • Internet Measurement: My research methodology often involves large-scale Internet measurements to gain deeper insights into network systems.
